About Jumbo's Pizza

Jumbo's Pizza is an authentic New York style pizza place brought to Tulsa, Oklahoma. Owner and operator Big Al grew up in New York City and spent 35 years learning under world famous Sal Marcano. It starts off with fresh never frozen ingredients and dough to bring his creations to perfection. It uses a wood fired brick oven to cook it to the right tempature so the cheese still drips off as you lift each slice.

New York style pizza is both thin and crispy not overwhelmed by sauce and toppings. One of the hallmarks of this style is the very large pieces that are eaten by folding it in half to keep the cheese from falling off the sides.

If you are needing a party catered or just looking for a pie for family dinner, we have got you covered.
